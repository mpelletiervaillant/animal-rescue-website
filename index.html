What we do

  • Get adoptable cats off the streets and into homes

    The stray cat problem in Ottawa is significant. So many of these animals are suitable for loving homes. We bring owners and pets together.

  • Manage stray colonies

    Feral cat colonies exist all over the city and are not managed, in part because there are no publicly accessible spay/neuter-and-release programs.

  • Foster stray cats

    We believe that any cat should have access to a loving home no matter their back story. We work closely will volunteers to help cats be adoption ready!
